01 / About

The Story Behind
the Builder

At 17, I'm not waiting for the future — I'm engineering it. Based in Algeria, I've spent the last five years transforming ideas into real, working systems that interact with the physical world.

My journey began with a curiosity about how things work — how a simple circuit can light up an LED, how code can bring a robot to life, how CAD software can turn a sketch into a 3D-printed component. That curiosity turned into passion, and passion turned into expertise.

Through the نبتكر (Nabtakir) association, I've pushed my boundaries in robotics and embedded systems. I work across the full hardware-software stack: from schematic design and PCB layout to Python scripts and ESP-based IoT devices. I'm also a confident public speaker who has presented innovations at national-level exhibitions attended by government ministers.

My goal is simple: to be an engineer who builds things that matter — for Algeria, for the Arab world, and for humanity.
